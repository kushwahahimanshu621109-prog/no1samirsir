About Us
Why Choose Samir Sir?

Samir Sir Coaching is Motihari's most trusted teaching centre for C.B.S.E. Board students. Since 2016, we have been delivering quality education in a fully English medium environment — helping students excel from Nursery right through to Class 12.

Our expert faculty, structured curriculum, and personalised attention ensure every student reaches their full potential — whether targeting board exams, Sainik School, BHU, or Navodaya Vidyalaya.
